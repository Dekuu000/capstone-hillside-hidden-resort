 2. Core Functional Modules 
Module A: Blockchain Reservation Ledger 
• Feature 1: Smart Contract Escrow: Automatically locks the guest's payment and 
room availability upon booking; releases funds only upon QR check-in. 
• Feature 2: NFT Guest Passes: Generates a unique, non-fungible token representing 
the booking that acts as the guest's digital "key." 
• Feature 3: Immutable Audit Trail: Every booking change or cancellation is logged 
permanently on the ledger to prevent "double-booking" disputes. 
Module B: QR-Based PWA Check-In 
• Feature 1: Offline Check-In Validation: The PWA stores a signed hash of the 
booking; guests can "check in" at the front desk even if the resort’s hillside Wi-Fi is 
temporarily down. 
• Feature 2: Dynamic QR Codes: Rotating QR codes that refresh every 30 seconds to 
prevent unauthorized entry sharing. 
• Feature 3: Resort Navigation: Offline-first interactive maps for guests to find 
"hidden" amenities without active GPS.

Module C: AI Hospitality Intelligence 
• Feature 1: Dynamic Pricing Engine: AI adjusts rates in real-time based on blockchain 
booking velocity, weather forecasts, and seasonal demand. 
• Feature 2: Personalized Concierge: Analyzes previous guest behavior (stored 
anonymously) to suggest tours or dining options. 
• Feature 3: Occupancy Forecasting: Predicts staff and inventory needs based on 
blockchain-confirmed arrival patterns. 
Module D: Resort Management Dashboard 
• Feature 1: Room Inventory Sync: Real-time view of room status (Cleaned, Occupied, 
Maintenance). 
• Feature 2: Ledger Explorer: A simplified view of all blockchain transactions for the 
resort accountant. 
• Feature 3: Resource Heatmap: Visualizes guest concentration to optimize cleaning 
and kitchen staff.

 4. System Flow Guide (The Developer’s Logic) 
1. Phase 1 (Booking): Guest selects a date on the PWA. The system triggers a Smart 
Contract transaction. The room is "locked" on the blockchain.

2. Phase 2 (Issuance): The system generates a Dynamic QR Code linked to the Guest's 
wallet address/booking hash. 
3. Phase 3 (Arrival): Guest arrives at the hillside resort. Even without Wi-Fi, the PWA 
displays the QR code from the local cache. 
4. Phase 4 (Validation): Resort staff scans the QR. The system verifies the Digital 
Signature against the blockchain record. 
5. Phase 5 (Check-In): Smart contract releases the room to "Occupied" status. AI 
triggers a welcome notification with personalized tour suggestions. 
6. Phase 6 (Settlement): Upon checkout, the smart contract completes the payment 
cycle and updates the resort's revenue ledger. 
 5. Security & Data Privacy 
• Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP): Use hashes for guest names on the blockchain to 
ensure privacy while maintaining transaction validity. 
• Wallet Integration: Support for MetaMask or custodial wallets for guests who aren't 
familiar with crypto. 
• Offline Security: The PWA uses AES-256 encryption for any guest data stored in the 
browser's IndexedDB.
